Ideal Jobs

Tommy: Web Developer

In order to achieve his ideal job as a web developer, Tommy will need to have knowledge of social media, e-commerce and mobile web as well as front-end coding skills such as HTML and CSS and experience in web design or related front-end coding. He also needs great communication and interpersonal skills since the job involves a lot of teamwork. To achieve this, he plans to graduate from RMIT with a bachelor’s degree in IT and find a graduate position to acquire experience.

Andy: Software Engineer

To get his ideal job as a software engineer at a big tech company such as Google, he will need to have knowledge of C, C++, Java or Python as well as at least two more coding languages. He will also need at least 4 years’ experience in the software development field. In obtain this, Andy plans to get a mentor in the field while conducting a lot of self-study and preparation for the coding questions that he expects to be met with in an interview.

Claire: Cybersecurity Analyst

To acquire her ideal job as a cybersecurity analyst, Claire will be required to have knowledge of ACSC Essential 8, ISO 27001&27002, NIST, MITRE ATT&CK, ITIL, understanding of network security, common vulnerabilities, database security, content filtering, vulnerability scanning and anti-malware and understanding of business needs. She will also need to have 2+ years of experience in the information security field. To achieve this, Claire plans to take courses related to cybersecurity and eventually graduate from RMIT with a Bachelor’s of IT and then find a graduate position to gain experience.

Matthew: IT Systems Administrator

In order to obtain his ideal job as a Systems Administrator, Matthew is required have a fundamental knowledge of the Microsoft 365 system and also understand how to use advance trouble shooting tools such as Wireshark and Procom. To achieve this, Matthew plans to undertake main subjects that will introduce him into the field of networking at RMIT and to then graduate with his Bachelor of IT and find a position to gain further experience.

Oliver: software Developer

Oliver broadened his ideal career to software development in general instead of is goal of games development after viewing the industry data. Software developers create software and apps. He will need to have knowledge of programming languages such as C++, Java and Python to write codes which executes the software and all its features etc. depending on the type.

Analysis

Each member of the group has a different ideal job and pathway currently. The categories of jobs could be split into two broader categories of ‘creating’ and ‘maintaining’, with web developer, software developer and software engineer being jobs in which the person makes new things exist, and IT systems administrator and cybersecurity analyst being jobs in which the person maintains and develops existing systems.
The ‘creating’ jobs all require some common skills which are coding languages such as C++ and JavaScript. IT administrator and cybersecurity analyst both require knowledge in software and frameworks specific to their field such as advanced troubleshooting and Wireshark in systems admin and MITRE ATT&CK for cybersecurity.
